K-marble

Morrocco

Grand Stone Enterprise is a marble company that specializes in the commercialization and export of all kinds of marbles and stones quarried in Morocco.

Based in Rabat, we capitalize on our extensive experience in the natural stone industry to find the best quality blocks extracted from any marble quarry in Morocco with a steady production line and quality in order to meet clients’ needs globally. In fact, we work with international stone consultants, architects, and contractors to advise and supply all types of Moroccan marbles and stones in either raw blocks, slabs, tiles, and/or cut to size; including massive objects, columns, or any other special decorative work.

We offer a wide variety of materials, namely: Grey Lido, Grey Saharas, Atlantic Grey, Snow Grey, Spider Grey, Nero Marquina, Moroccan Bluestone, Nero Saint Laurent, Nero Port Laurent, Black Fossil, Brown Fossil, Sunset Red, Rose S’houl, Light Travertine, Travertine Noche, Green Limestone, Grey Limestone, Beige Limestone, and the Yellow California.
